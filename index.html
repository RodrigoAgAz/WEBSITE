Medication Refill Reminder

Anna identifies members due for refills and proactively reaches out

A
Hi Robert! This is Anna from your Medicare plan. I noticed your blood pressure medication (Lisinopril) is due for a refill. Would you like me to help arrange that with your pharmacy?
2:15 PM
I've been feeling fine lately. Do I still need to take it?
2:18 PM
A
It's great that you're feeling well! That often means your medication is working effectively. Blood pressure medications work preventively - stopping them can lead to complications even if you feel fine. Would you like to discuss this with your doctor first, or shall I go ahead with the refill?
2:18 PM
Ok that makes sense. Please refill it.
2:20 PM
A
Perfect! I've sent the refill request to CVS on Main Street. It will be ready for pickup tomorrow after 10 AM. I'll text you a reminder. Is there anything else I can help you with today?
2:20 PM

Impact on Your Plan:

  • Improves medication adherence rates (key STAR measure)
  • Prevents complications from discontinued medications
  • Reduces inbound calls to your care management team

Annual Wellness Visit Scheduling

Anna ensures members complete their important annual check-ups

A
Hi James! This is Anna from your Medicare plan. It's time for your Annual Wellness Visit - a comprehensive check-up that's completely free with your plan. This visit helps your doctor create a personalized prevention plan. Can I help schedule this for you?
3:00 PM
What does that include?
3:15 PM
A
Great question! Your Annual Wellness Visit includes: review of your medical history, checking vital signs like blood pressure, discussing preventive services you may need, updating your medication list, and creating a plan to keep you healthy. It typically takes about 30-45 minutes. Would you like to schedule it with Dr. Thompson?
3:15 PM

Impact on Your Plan:

  • Increases Annual Wellness Visit completion (STAR measure)
  • Helps identify new conditions for proper risk adjustment
  • Strengthens member-provider relationships

Chronic Condition Management

Anna provides ongoing support for members with chronic conditions

A
Good morning Susan! This is Anna checking in on your diabetes management. How have your blood sugar levels been this week?
9:00 AM
They've been running a bit high, around 180-200
9:10 AM
A
Thank you for sharing that. Those levels are higher than your target range. Have you been taking your Metformin as prescribed? Any changes to your diet or activity level recently?
9:10 AM
I've been stressed and eating more. Also forgot my medicine a few times
9:12 AM
A
I understand - stress can definitely affect blood sugar. Would you like me to: 1) Set up daily medication reminders on your phone? 2) Schedule a visit with your diabetes educator for stress management tips? 3) Connect you with our nutritionist for meal planning support? Any or all of these could help!
9:12 AM

Impact on Your Plan:

  • Reduces hospital admissions and ER visits
  • Improves diabetes care metrics (HbA1c control)
  • Provides early intervention before conditions worsen
