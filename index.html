Core Features

Anna's Capabilities

Comprehensive tools designed to enhance Medicare Advantage performance through SMS-based patient engagement

Preventive Care Engagement

Sends personalized reminders for screenings like mammograms and colonoscopies while educating patients based on their medical history.

Medication Adherence

Tracks refills, sends reminders, detects non-adherence patterns, and provides contextual education on medications.

Appointment Coordination

Sends reminders, assists with rescheduling via SMS, and follows up post-appointment to check recommendation adherence.

Chronic Condition Management

Engages patients with conditions like diabetes and hypertension, using NLP to escalate issues when needed.

STAR Rating Optimization

Targets patients who impact HEDIS & CAHPS scores, increases wellness visit compliance, and enhances member experience.

AI-Powered Conversations

Experience AI-Powered Patient Conversations

Hello James, this is Anna from HealthFirst Medicare. I noticed you haven't refilled your blood pressure medication this month. Would you like me to help with that?
I've been feeling fine lately. Do I really need to keep taking it?
That's actually a common question. Even when you feel fine, your blood pressure medication is working to prevent complications. Your doctor recommended consistent use based on your health history. Would you like me to schedule your refill for pickup at Central Pharmacy or delivery to your home?
Delivery would be better. I also have some questions about side effects.
I'll arrange home delivery for tomorrow. Regarding side effects, your medication may cause mild dizziness when standing up quickly. Would you like me to connect you with a pharmacist for a more detailed discussion, or schedule a check-in with your primary care provider?
