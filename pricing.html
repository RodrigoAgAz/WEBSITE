Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

We bill monthly based on your active member count. You only pay for members who receive at least one engagement from Anna each month.

Can we customize the messaging?

Absolutely. Anna's conversations are fully customizable to match your brand voice and specific clinical protocols.

What about members without smartphones?

Anna works with basic SMS text messaging and voice calls, reaching 98% of Medicare beneficiaries.

How long does implementation take?

Most plans go live within 4-6 weeks, including EHR integration, testing, and staff training.

Is there a contract minimum?

Pilot programs require a 3-month commitment. Full deployments are typically annual contracts with monthly billing.

Can we start with a specific population?

Yes! Many plans start with high-risk diabetics or members with multiple chronic conditions to prove ROI quickly.
